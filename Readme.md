# Agri Trade – Agricultural Marketplace Mobile App

---

## Overview
**Agri Trade** is a modern **mobile marketplace application designed for farmers and agricultural traders** to buy and sell farming equipment, seeds, and agricultural products.

The platform connects **farmers directly with buyers**, removing intermediaries and making agricultural trading **simple, transparent, and efficient**.

With a clean UI, product listings, seller profiles, and marketplace features, the app enables users to **list farm products, browse listings, contact sellers, and manage sales activity**.

---

## Problem Statement

Farmers often face difficulties when trying to sell or purchase agricultural equipment and products due to:

- Limited access to digital marketplaces  
- Lack of direct farmer-to-buyer communication  
- Middlemen increasing product costs  
- No centralized marketplace for agriculture tools and products  

**Agri Trade solves this by providing:**

- A dedicated agriculture marketplace  
- Direct communication between buyers and sellers  
- Easy product listing and browsing  
- Mobile-first design for accessibility
